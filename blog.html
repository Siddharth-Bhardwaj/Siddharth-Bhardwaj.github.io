Awards & Publications

NBA an award-winning firm at various platforms has time and again proved its excellence in architecture designing and interior works. It has won awards on platforms like IIID – AWARD (ZONAL Awards), India and A DESIGN – AWARD, Milan, Italy. With remarkable projects under its name, NBA has paved the way for design being timeless and elegant in a range of design formats.

NBA has even scaled its journey on the platform of publications in various publication houses such as Inside Outside, API Magazine, The Top Most, Buildcon Architecture and Interior Exterior with a range of projects and different nature. These publications display the sheer zeal of passion towards architecture driven by quality in the built structures.
