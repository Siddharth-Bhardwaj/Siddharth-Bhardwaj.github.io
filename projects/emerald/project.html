Emerald

Vadodara

Emerald

We created appealing, sustainable workplaces that ingeniously bridges context with their needs and technologies. We merge our understanding of their culture and expertise in optimising functionality to design work environments that build joy, communities and efficiency. Greens in the waiting area as well as the partitions accompanied with glass and metal frames gives visual porosity.

This project creates a clutter free yet element working area with nude tones and edgy furniture. By combining the space with a glass surface on one of the sides it gives and added volume additionally diving the area into three zones. Office cabins partitions are designed using corten steel, metal cladding patterns and siporex blocks in a way to create a minimalistic and elegant space.

The materials are carried forward in the furniture designs as well. The whole office has exposed ceiling and hanging profile lights in different shapes adding a very rustic feeling to the office.
