Jhalak Showroom

The important feature of an Indian function is flower, be it in decoration or in the attire. Keeping it as a centralized concept of the design, it has been enhanced in Jhalak showroom. The approach for this space was to render the ambience with similar tone of colour and draw attention to the numerous colours and textures of the fabrics.

The ground floor has a combination of white and grey tones of colour, while the first floor comprises white with brown tones.

The space of this floor is richly layered and textured with a thoughtful mix of wood in sliders, floor and customised jail partitions, which extends into the choice of furniture, furnishings and artwork. Flower patterns is used extensively to enhance the ambience and give an Indian fell to the space.
