Utopian

Vadodara

Utopian

Situated in the prime location of Vadodara, theÂ flat is no less than a villa on a level with vast spaces blending into each other flawlessly. Entrance foyer welcomes guest with earthy bricks, brown Kota stone flooring and moss green wall adding up to the ambiance with intricate details. A transition space with stone cladding gives a sense of orientation towards different spaces becoming the focal point itself.

Living room has vibrant colours juxtaposed against subtle background with accent lighting.The interconnectedness of living room with other areas allows for a grand and seamless living space.The combination of metal and wood has been taken into consideration while detailing out the dining and kitchen area.

The master bedroom follows the subdued aesthetic of the home and is kept very spacious with open glass washroom area along with green pockets which binds the spaces together in a flawless manner. Whilst the adjoining bedroom is in the tones of blue & white, with upholstered blue fabric, adding playfulness to the space.
