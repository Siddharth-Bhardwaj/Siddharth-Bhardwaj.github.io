JSK Showroom

Vadodara

JSK Showroom

“Elegance, sophistication and simplicity”- This is what the design portrays & client envisioned. As one enters, the space creates a whole scenario with its minimal design & material replicated throughout the whole house. Starting with the main door with its unique design & geometric patterns crafted in wood, the space is an open plan revealing the living room on one side & kitchen & dining on the other side.

Stepping into the foyer, Positive vibes are felt from a see through wooden jali accompanied with hanging bells, opening a view to the pooja room, furthermore connecting to the soothing color palette of the living room. The dining area builds a gateway of entry by seamlessly combining the indoors and the outdoors forming an overwhelming ambience. The area lives up to having an individual aura of its own design character.

Bringing in the geometric patter inside the bedrooms; beat it cupboard shutters, blinds or bed base, the language was followed giving a holistic look & maintaining a simplistic yet alluring interiors.
