The Vogue House

Vadodara

The Vogue House

Fashion designers, Dinesh Khangani and Hina Khangani, wanted their home to be bold and minimalistic with abundance of art. Both being health conscious, the elevator was avoided and lift well converted into an art well. Imposing bronze sculpture of young elated girl perched on the podium highlights the art corner along the steps.

The sumptuous double-height space is adorned with 48 clay emojis with unique expressions on one side and delicate metal partition on the opposite side. Catering to the influence of fashion in our clients' taste, there is a quirky edge to the space by adding bold hues to the furniture and fabrics. Big openings across the home ensure a sense of openness and connect inside with the outside spaces.
