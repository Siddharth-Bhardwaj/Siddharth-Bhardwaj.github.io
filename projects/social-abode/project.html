The Social Abode

'Live life king-size' our clientâ€™s lives by this mantra. The design was driven by the fact that the dynamic couple, love to entertain and socialize with family and friends. The social core of the house, swimming pool area with glass floor above to accommodate pool parties, a discotheque cum A.V room and poker room are specifically designed to cater for a variety of gatherings. The design scheme utilizes dynamic interplay between light, colours and textures to strike a balance between vibrant social spaces and serene private zones to provide a diverse spatial experience.

Merging the indoors with outdoors, the main social area consists of an enormous pool deck connected to the living space and other social pockets.

The double heighted living space projected with glass box, specially crafted to appreciate the custom made chandelier and intricate wooden screen which similarly continues the same language for the puja room.
